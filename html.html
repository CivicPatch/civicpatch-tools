Public Records Request

The City of Maple Valley does not maintain Police records and/or court records.

To request Police records please contact King County Sheriff's Office at 206-296-4190.

To request court records please contact the City of Kent Municipal Court at 253-856-5730.


The City of Maple Valley, in accordance with the Washington State Public Records Act, allows for the inspection or copies of records unless specifically exempted by law.

The city has adopted rules and regulations to carry out the public disclosure requirements of Chapter 42.56 RCW, which are codified in Maple Valley Municipal Code 2.50.
