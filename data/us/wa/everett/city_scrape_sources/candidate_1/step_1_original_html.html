Mayor's Office

Learn more about Mayor Franklin

Mayor Cassie Franklin took office on Jan. 1, 2018. Learn more about her and her vision for Everett. 

Mayor Franklin’s priorities

Mayor Franklin's priorities are violence prevention, behavioral health and creating a prosperous city. To learn more about these critical priorities and how they guide the City’s work, check out her annual State of Everett address and her Mayoral Directives.
