Responsibilities

The Land Use & Transportation Committee (LUTC) is a City Council Committee that reviews issues regarding land use, streets, and traffic. The Planning Commission and Historic Landmark Commission, Council-appointed commissions, report to the LUTC. Meetings are subject to change.
